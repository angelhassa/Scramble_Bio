Scramble for Biologists

Instructions

A biology term has been scrambled.

Your mission should you choose to accept it, is to figure out what that term is in 60 seconds.

Your side quest is to make as many English words as possible using the letters.

To help you along in your mission the first letter of the biology term has been capiatalised

Words are checked only after the timer ends.

At the end of the round, the biology term and its definition will be revealed.
